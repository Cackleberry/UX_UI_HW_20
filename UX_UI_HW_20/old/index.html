Need to eat late? Here's a food delivery option

Students and night workers cannot grocery shop after work as stores are closed - this causes, frustration and results in snacking and eating fast food at night or not eating at all; enter Better Bites - a solution, to deliver, late at night (or early in the AM), food tailored to our users needs.

Qualitative data was gleaned through a survey (online forums - such as Reddit subgroups), social media (Facebook, Instagram) and via email. In conjunction with the survey, interviews were also conducted to learn about users pain points.
